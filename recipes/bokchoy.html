Bok Choy

Sauteed Bok Choy... the perfect veggie side dish

This recipe goes well with pretty much everything

Ingredients

  • 2-3 baby bok choy
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut bok choy into 1 inch strips lengthwise
  2. Dice garlic
  3. Add oil (coconut oil recommended) to large pan over medium-high heat, then add cut bok choy
  4. Cook until bok choy begins getting soft, 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently
  5. Add salt and pepper and garlic and cook for additional 1-2 minutes, stirring frequently
  6. Enjoy!
