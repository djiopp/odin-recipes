Rice

Rice... what more needs to be said?

It's just white rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice of choice
  • 2 cups water
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Add rice, water, and salt to medium sauce pan
  2. Bring to a boil
  3. Reduce heat to low and cover
  4. Let simmer for 15 minutes+. DO NOT LIFT THE COVER.
  5. Fluff rice with a fork or spatula or whatever and serve
  6. Enjoy!
